Empowering Africa’s Next Generation of Cybersecurity Leaders

Our Story

The Root Access Network (TRAN) began as a mentorship initiative dedicated to helping young Nigerians and Africans break into IT and Cybersecurity. What started as a small group of passionate mentors has now grown into a thriving community of learners, professionals, and advocates spreading cyber awareness across schools, streets, and cities.

From the busy streets of Lagos to classrooms across Africa, TRAN is building a culture of digital literacy, mentorship, and inclusion. Our mission is to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible to everyone.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To empower students and professionals with mentorship, education, and real-world cybersecurity skills.

Vision

A future where cybersecurity knowledge is accessible to everyone, regardless of background or resources.

Values

Community • Education • Collaboration • Integrity • Inclusion
